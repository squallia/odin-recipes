Biscuits & Gravy

Ingredients

  • Canned Biscuits
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Chipped Beef

Directions

  1. Get your biscuits in the oven
  2. Fry up the chipped beef in butter
  3. Add flour (about a quarter cup), until all the butter is congealed
  4. Add water and simmer until gravy is thick
